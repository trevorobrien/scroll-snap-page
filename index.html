    OUR ETHOS

    True Sustainability, Now Possible

    Eon is recycling clothes , reusing materials and renting clothes to reduce waste and the demand for new natural resources. Our goal is to go zero waste, so that materials can be continuously reused. By using no new resources, and going zero waste — we can achieve true sustainability. Truly sustainable fashion is circular. Our mission is to support the fashion industry to be fully circular, to close the loop. Learn more about circular fashion.
